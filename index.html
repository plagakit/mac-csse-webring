▶ What's a webring?

A webring is a collection of websites linked together in a ring. Each site has links to the previous and next site, so visitors can move through the ring. This directory lists McMaster CS & SE students and alumni who share their sites and portfolios. Joining is free: add your site via a pull request to the repo.
